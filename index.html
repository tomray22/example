About

Though there are many programming clubs like it GWTP is one of a kind.

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Two friends, Ethan Mendoza and Ray Cruz thought to themselves "We should make a club", and so they did.

  • DECEMBER 2015

    A Club is Born

    Off to a shaky start the pair were able to garner members but had no idea how to run a club

  • MARCH 2016

    A Revival

    Over time in the last quarter of the year they lost more and more members until Ray decided to make a game himself. This garnered the attention of a few dedicated members and despite not having a succesful release it raised morale for next year.

  • SEPTEMBER 2016

    Phase Two Expansion

    Even with the loss of the former President and Co-Founder Ethan, Ray is continuing the club and improving it through the lessons learned from past experiences.
